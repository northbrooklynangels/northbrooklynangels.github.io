# Neighbors helping neighbors in Greenpoint, Williamsburg, and Bushwick - since 2017.

We're North Brooklyn Angels, a volunteer-powered nonprofit serving our community through three year-round programs and one beloved twice-yearly tradition:

* **Mobile Soup Kitchen** - 2,000 free hot meals every week, 7 days a week, no questions asked.
* **Little Angels** - baby gear for local parents who need it, delivered to your door.
* **Live Local** - free help navigating NYC's affordable housing lottery, for Greenpoint and Williamsburg residents.
* **Angel Babies** - twice a year, we open our doors for a community giveaway of clothing, toys, and essentials for local families.

750,000+ meals served · 950+ volunteers a year · 75+ families equipped through Little Angels

All of it powered by two full-time staff, a handful of dedicated part-timers, and a community that shows up every single day.
