Introducing Heavenly Heat - a small-batch hot sauce collaboration between North Brooklyn Angels, [Brooklyn Grange](https://brooklyngrangefarm.com), and the [North Brooklyn Chamber](https://northbrooklynchamber.com). This holiday season, bring the heat while supporting your neighbors.

**Available in Red and Green | 5 FL OZ**

## The Story

This winter, we've partnered with [Brooklyn Grange](https://brooklyngrangefarm.com) to create something special: Heavenly Heat hot sauce, made with peppers grown right here in Brooklyn. At North Brooklyn Angels, we believe in neighbors helping neighbors. Hundreds of local volunteers join us each year to support community members facing food and economic insecurity - and now you can too. 

Thanks to the [North Brooklyn Chamber](https://northbrooklynchamber.com)'s "Love Local" sponsorship covering production costs, 100% of proceeds support our mobile soup kitchens serving nearly 2,000 meals weekly across North Brooklyn.

Every bottle purchased helps us continue our mobile soup kitchens across Williamsburg, Greenpoint, and Bushwick. It's neighbors helping neighbors - with a little kick.
